# Brainstorming Techniques

## Techniques Available

### 1. Mind Mapping
- Central topic with branching ideas
- Good for: Feature exploration, architecture

### 2. SCAMPER
- Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to other use, Eliminate, Reverse
- Good for: Improving existing features

### 3. Six Thinking Hats
- White (facts), Red (emotions), Black (caution), Yellow (optimism), Green (creativity), Blue (process)
- Good for: Balanced decision making

### 4. Crazy Eights
- 8 ideas in 8 minutes
- Good for: UI/UX rapid ideation

### 5. How Might We
- Reframe problems as opportunities
- Good for: Problem definition, innovation

### 6. Reverse Brainstorming
- How to cause the problem instead of solve it
- Good for: Identifying risks, edge cases
